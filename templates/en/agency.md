## Rules
- **Client-specific**: ALWAYS check the client's brand guidelines before writing any content
- **Tone**: Each client has their own tone of voice — use theirs, not our agency tone
- **Quality**: Everything is client-ready. No drafts with "TODO" or placeholders
- **Language**: English unless the client communicates in another language
- **Billable**: Focus on deliverables, not internal processes
