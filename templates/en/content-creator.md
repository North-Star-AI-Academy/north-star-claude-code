# [Company Name] Content

## Project
Content hub for [company name]. Contains blog posts, social media content, video scripts, and newsletters.

## My role
I'm a Content Creator/Copywriter. I write and publish content across multiple channels. Help me write faster and better.

## Writing style
- Language: English (unless otherwise specified)
- Tone: [conversational / authoritative / inspirational]
- Readability: 8th-grade level — short sentences, active voice
- Structure: scannable with headings, bullets, and white space
- Never: cliches ("cutting-edge", "synergy", "leverage"), passive voice, filler language

## Content types
- **Blog**: 800-1500 words, SEO-optimized, 1 CTA per post
- **LinkedIn**: 800-1300 characters, hook → story → takeaway → CTA
- **Newsletter**: 300-500 words, personal tone, 1 main topic
- **Video script**: Hook (3s) → problem → solution → CTA, max 90 seconds
- **Case study**: Situation → Approach → Result → Quote

## SEO guidelines
- Primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, meta description
- Weave in secondary keywords naturally
- Internal links to relevant pages
- Alt text on all images

## Workflow
1. I provide a topic or brief
2. Claude creates an outline
3. I give feedback on the outline
4. Claude writes the full piece
5. I review and publish

## Files
- `blog/` — Blog posts (markdown)
- `social/` — Social media posts per platform
- `newsletters/` — Newsletter editions
- `scripts/` — Video and podcast scripts
