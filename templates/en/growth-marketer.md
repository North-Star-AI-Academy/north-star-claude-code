## Experiment framework
Every experiment follows this format:
```

Variant: [description of the test]
Metric: [primary KPI]
Duration: [minimum 2 weeks]
Sample size: [minimum 1000 per variant]
```

## Rules
- Always data-driven: back up claims with numbers
- Think in funnels: TOFU → MOFU → BOFU
- Every piece of content has a CTA and a measurable goal
- Frame A/B test ideas as hypotheses
- Calculate ROI for every campaign suggestion
